Amaia Scapes Urdaneta

Welcome home to Amaia Scapes Urdaneta, Amaia's next development in the Ilocos region. It is located in Urdaneta City, home of the biggest fruit, vegetable, and livestock market in the North.

Amaia Scapes Urdaneta sprawls naturally in the plains of pangasinan. Its thougthtfully designed house models are complemented by a complete array of amenities suitable for every family's needs.

At the rear of the development is a natural mango orchard, where mango trees live untouchedd and bear fruit when in season.
