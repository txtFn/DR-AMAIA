Amaia Scapes Cagayan de Oro

Amaia Scapes aims to set a new standard in economic housing by providing Filipino families with homes that are within their means without compromising the quality. Amaia Scapes promises affordable, secured and innovatively-designed homes.

Amaia Scapes Cagayan de Oro is the first project of Amaia Land Corporation in Misamis Oriental. It is located at Macapagal Drive, Brgy. Balua, Cagayan De Oro City.

Price Range: Php1M to Php3M
Land Area: 18,290m2 / 3,204.32m2
Type of Terrain: Relatively Flat
No. of Units: 703 Units
Location: Brgy. Balua, Cagayan de Oro City

Amaia Scapes Cagayan de Oro LTS Number: Sector 1A 030202 / Sector 1B 030203
