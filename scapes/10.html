Amaia Scapes Iloilo

Amaia Scapes is an economic housing development that caters to Filipinos who want to upgrade their status in life. Amaia Scapes aims to set a new standard in economic housing by providing Filipino families with homes that are within their means without compromising the quality. Amaia Scapes promises affordable, secured and innovatively-designed homes.

Amaia Scapes Iloilo is the first project of Amaia Land Corp. in Iloilo. It is located at Brgy. San Jose, San Miguel, Iloilo.

Price Range: Php1.4M to Php3M
Project Land Area: 15 Hectares (approx.)/ 15,000 sqm
Type of Terrain: Relatively Flat
No. of Units: 980 Units
Location: Brgy. San Jose, San Miguel, Iloilo

Amaia Scapes Iloilo LTS No. Secto 1A 030116 / Sector 1B 030117
