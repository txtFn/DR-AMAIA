Amaia Steps The Junction Place

Address: Quirino Highway and Tandang Sora Street, Quezon City
Amaia Steps The Junction Place is located along Quirino Highway Novaliches, Quezon City.
The Junction Place Estate is a 10-hectare mixed use development designed for those who seek a break from the routine and aspires a better self. The estate is bounded by Quirino Highway and Tandang Sora Avenue and will feature retail establishments, office buildings, events venue along with other establishments.

Price Range: Php3.2M to Php4.1M
Project Land Area: 1.7 Hectares
No. of Buildings: 5
No. of Floors: 9
Residential Units: 2nd to 9th Floor
Parking Units: Ground Floor
Sizes of Units: 23.83 sqm to 31.87 sqm
DHSUD LS: 014
