Amaia Steps Altaraza

Amaia now ventures on its first mid-rise project in Altaraza Town Center, a 55-hectare township project in Bulacan, with Amaia Steps Altaraza. Amaia Steps Altaraza is the first affordable mid-rise development situated in Altaraza Town Center, designed to offer a vibrant city living more hardworking Filipinos, who dream of owning a home for their families.

Take it easy knowing that our community is managed by Ayala Property Management Corporation.

At Amaia Land, easy and flexible payment terms are offered, so you can finally have the chance to have your own home. Step into a vibrant city living!

Price Range: Php1.6M to Php3.2M

Project Area: 1.3 Hectares / 13,000 sqm
Size of Units: 23 - 56 sqm
No. of Buildings: 4 (Phase 1)
No. of Floors: 8 (Aria), 9 (Blanca, Clara and Delicia)
No. of Residential Units: 998
