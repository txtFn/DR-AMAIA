Amaia Steps Capitol Central

Amaia Steps Capitol Central is the first mid-rise residential development of Amaia Land Corp. in the City of Smiles, Bacolod. It enjoys the previlege of having its own support retail shops for the convenience of its future residents.

Featuring its South Building with 9-storey development, Amaia Steps Capitol Central will have 276 units of well designed Studio, De luxe, and Premier units. Some units are afforded balconies that give access to the tropical views of the open spaces and the city.

At the core of the development is an invigorating lifestyle amenity courtyard, which includes swimming pools for adults and kids, kids' play area, lanscaped garden, and a multi-purpose hall.
